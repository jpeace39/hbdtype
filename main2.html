Raspberry Pi . . .

The Raspberry Pi is a low cost, credit-card sized computer that plugs into a computer monitor or TV,

and uses a standard keyboard and mouse.

It is a capable little device that enables people of all ages to explore computing,

and to learn how to program in languages like Scratch and Python.

Sonic Pi . . .

Sonic Pi is an open source program that allows you to create new sounds in a live coding environment. It is a new musical instrument that is played through the writing of code rather than the stroking of keys or strumming of strings. https://sonic-pi.net/
